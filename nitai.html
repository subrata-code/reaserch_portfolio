Dr. Nitai Chandra Adak

Experimental Mechanics & Composites Researcher

About Me

Dr. Adak's research focuses on experimental mechanics of advanced multifunctional composites. He has developed graphene-hybrid nanocomposites for EMI shielding, epoxy-based structural electrolytes for energy storage, and thermally stable carbon/aramid/epoxy composites for high-temperature applications. His work includes filament-wound Type-IV cylinders for hydrogen storage. Currently, he is investigating 3D-printed FRP composites under varied strain rates and magnetoelectric nanocomposite inks for wearable electronics and soft robotics applications.
